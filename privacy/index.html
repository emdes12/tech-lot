PRIVACY POLICY


At Tech-lot, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information and data that we receive from our customers and visitors.

We may collect personal information that you provide to us when you contact us, create an account on our website, or use our services. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and other information that you provide to us.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION


We use personal information that we collect to provide and improve our services, to communicate with our customers, and to comply with legal obligations. We may also use personal information to conduct research and analysis to improve our services and to customize your experience on our website.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION


We may disclose personal information to our partners, service providers, and affiliates as necessary to provide and improve our services. We may also disclose personal information as required by law or in response to legal process, including government requests or subpoenas.
